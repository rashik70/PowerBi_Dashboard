# PowerBi_Dashboard

# Project Title
Meta Ads Performance Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

# Short Description / Purpose
An end‑to‑end analytics dashboard built in Power BI to evaluate the performance of Meta (Facebook + Instagram) paid advertising campaigns. The dashboard helps marketing teams measure reach, engagement, conversions, and budget efficiency while identifying high‑value audiences, best‑performing ad formats, and optimal scheduling times.

# Tech Stack
• Power BI
• DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
• Power Query
• Excel / CSV Data Files
• Star Schema Data Modeling
• Data Visualization & KPI Design

# Data Source
Dataset modeled on Meta Ads event‑level logs, ad metadata, user demographics, and campaign budgets including:
• ad_events (fact table – impressions, clicks, shares, comments, purchases)
• ads (ad type, platform, targeting)
• campaigns (budget, duration, campaign details)
• users (gender, age, country, interests)

# Features / Highlights

 **Business Problem**
Marketing teams lacked visibility into the ad funnel, making it difficult to track ROI, optimize spend, and understand who engages with campaigns and why conversions remain low despite strong impressions.

 **Goal of the Dashboard**
To provide a unified performance view across awareness, engagement, and conversions, enabling better budget decisions, audience targeting, and optimization strategies.

 **Walkthrough of Key Visuals (briefly)**
  • KPI Summary Cards → Impressions, Clicks, Shares, Comments, Purchases, CTR, Engagement Rate, Conversion Rate, Budget metrics
  • Funnel Metrics → Awareness to click to purchase drop‑off analysis
  • Gender/Age Insights → Identify top‑engaging demographics
  • Country Heat Map → Understand high‑volume vs high‑value markets
  • Weekly & Hourly Trends → Reveal best days and hours for ad scheduling
  • Calendar Heatmap → Identify promotional spikes
  • Ad Type Matrix → Compare performance of Videos, Stories, Images, Carousel ads

**Business Impact & Insights**
  • CTR of 11.76% shows extremely strong ad creatives and targeting.
  • Engagement rate is high, but purchases are low (0.61% of impressions), indicating funnel leakage.
  • Best‑performing audience: Females 18–30 from India & Brazil.
  • Video & Story formats outperform Image/Carousel ads across CTR, conversion, and engagement metrics.
  • Optimal ad timing: Afternoon & evening hours.
  • High‑value regions: Germany & UK (higher purchasing power).
